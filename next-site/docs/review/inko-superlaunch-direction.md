# Inko Super Launch Direction

## Compared Directions

### Dark Launch
- Stronger first-impression pull
- Better product-launch energy
- Better fit for the current dark-stage hero and animated workflow
- Stronger pricing gravity and more credible "complete system" feel

### Quiet Authority Luxe
- More restrained and service-premium
- Slightly stronger on calm trust
- Better if the brand wanted to stay mostly editorial and less launch-like
- Slightly weaker on immediate desire and visual magnetism

## Recommendation

Choose `Dark Launch`.

It is the stronger commercial option because it:
- makes the hardware and software feel like one staged product system
- gives the homepage a clearer release-event presence
- supports richer workflow storytelling without changing the information architecture
- makes the pricing area feel more like a decisive buying surface than a content section
- helps Proof read as a real app shell rather than a gallery of examples

## Implementation Notes

- Keep the current page structure and route structure.
- Keep checkout in placeholder/review mode until the live URL is provided.
- Use Framer Motion for layered stage reveal, parallax, mode transitions, and workflow micro-motion.
- Keep support email as `hello@get-inko.com`.
- Continue using config-driven content and asset references.
