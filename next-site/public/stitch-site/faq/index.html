Product basics

What is Inko Starter? expand_more
Inko Starter is an ambient intelligence hardware-software hybrid designed for professional advisors. It sits in your workspace and autonomously transcribes, synthesizes, and logs client conversations directly into your CRM with zero manual input.
How does the hardware interact with my existing tools? expand_more
Inko connects via encrypted WiFi to our cloud engine, which bridges into 40+ professional CRMs including Salesforce, Wealthbox, and Redtail. It functions as a secure peripheral that feeds your existing ecosystem.

Who it is for

Is Inko designed for solo practitioners or large firms? expand_more
Both. While Inko Starter is optimized for individual desks, our Enterprise layer allows compliance officers to monitor logs across entire branches.

Privacy and consent

Zero-Storage Policy

Audio is processed in ephemeral memory and immediately deleted after transcription. We never store raw audio files on our servers or your device.

Is Inko SOC2 Compliant? expand_more
Yes. Our backend architecture is SOC2 Type II compliant and meets GDPR requirements for data handling and client consent management. We provide template consent language for your client agreements.
